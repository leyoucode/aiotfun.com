## What it is

A GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) platform — monitor how a brand surfaces across search engines, AI assistants, and content platforms, then output actionable suggestions.

## Hard parts

It is not just scraping:

- **Source coverage**: different search engines and different AI assistants (Doubao / Kimi / Perplexity / etc.) give different answers to the same query — all of them need to be ingested.
- **Dedup**: a single news item gets republished by dozens of sites; you need to detect "different framings of the same event."
- **Sentiment**: classic positive/negative classification is not enough. You need finer signals ("praised" vs "cited as a counterexample" vs "mentioned neutrally").
- **Actionability**: telling a user "your brand performs poorly on AI X" is useless. You need to tell them **why**, and **what to change**.

## What I worked on

- Multi-source ingestion + dedup pipeline (SimHash + content hash + time-window heuristics).
- Embedding LLMs into the analysis pipeline — not as a chatbot, but as "an intern who reads articles": classify, extract, judge tone.
- Recommendation generator that maps analysis results to concrete SEO / content actions.
